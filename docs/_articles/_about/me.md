## scitia etymology

Scythia was founded on May 25, 1924. It brought together students of the Lviv Polytechnic. 

A particular aim of the corporation was to work for the defense of Polish identity 

in the Eastern Borderlands of the Republic. The name of the corporation referred to the ancient

inhabitants of the Black Sea steppes—sometimes regarded as proto-ancestors of the Poles. 

My great-grandfather was one of its members, which I commemorate by bearing this nickname.
